About Me

I have over 15 years of experience working with educators, faculty, and eLearning professionals in various positions. My roles have included being a Director, Instructional Designer, Educational Presenter and a secondary educator. My skills and professional interests are in incorporating researched based pedagogy, with sound instructional methods, within eLearning and blended learning environments. I am also an ardent user of a wide variety of technologies and software programs. Adobe Creative Suite, Camtasia and Articulate Storyline are just a few that I have used.

Future Goals

As I deepen my web development expertise and skill set I want to be able to implement my newly earned skills in my current position. Daily I work in developing curriculum, interactives, as well as the way students interact with content in the online environment. Being able to enhance the student experience with these items will be made possible from the development of my coding skills. One example could be a Personal Finance course could using an app that could log expenses and create budgets for different spending categories (entertainment, food, etc.). Another could be an Economics course could utilizing an app linked to the Trading Economics API. This API provides access to real time information on the financial markets. Students could engage in an authentic assessment using in the moment information and apply the information to the assessment at hand. Yet another could be involvement in the development a new Student Information System for my organization. This is the system that integrates with the LMS providing enrollments, grades, course offerings and much more. As I advance in my skills I will be able to contribute to the creation and maintenance of the SIS.
